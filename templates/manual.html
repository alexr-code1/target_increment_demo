Trivial Compute Manual

Welcome to Trivial Compute! This is a simple web-based board game where players roll a dice and move on a grid. The game includes the following features:

  • Players can click the "Roll Dice" button to roll a dice and move their pieces accordingly.
  • If a player lands on the "Trivial Compute" cell (E5), they will win the game!
  • Players get a score of 0.5 if they land on E5 again and win.
  • Players get a score of 1 if they land past (5, 5) at the end of their turn.
  • Players can choose a direction (up, down, left, or right) to move after rolling the dice.
  • Players can answer a trivia question after moving, and their answers will be checked for correctness.
  • Correct answers increase the player's score, while incorrect answers move the game to the next player.

Enjoy playing Trivial Compute with your friends! If you have any questions or issues, please refer to the game manual or contact our support team.
